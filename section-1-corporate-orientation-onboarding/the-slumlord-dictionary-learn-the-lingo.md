# 📖 The Slumlord Dictionary: Learn the Lingo

**"Learn the language of extraction, Junior. If you can't define the grift, you become the mark."**

Welcome to the BlackCoq dictionary. In this business, we don't use words like "community," "sustainability," or "fairness." We use words that look great on a quarterly earnings report. Memorize this terminology before you step onto the trading floor.

***

<details>

<summary><strong>Auto-Eject (The Mass Eviction)</strong></summary>

The protocol's violent automated defense mechanism, triggered when you execute a Skill Point reset. It forcefully and immediately unstakes every single property in your portfolio, claims your pending yield, and physically rips the upgrades off the walls to prevent you from exploiting your staking capacity.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>BPS (Basis Points)</strong></summary>

The standard metric of our greed. 1 BPS = 0.01%. We use this inside the smart contracts because telling the city council we are raising fees by "2500 BPS" sounds like complex economics, whereas saying "we're hiking the rent by 25%" sounds like a crime.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Dead Capital</strong></summary>

Any property or upgrade currently rotting in your local `WALLET` tab. If it isn't actively staked inside the `PORTFOLIO` contract draining a tenant's life savings, it is dead weight. Holding dead capital is a fireable offense at BlackCoq.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Fatigue (Planned Obsolescence)</strong></summary>

The inevitable structural decay of your concrete boxes. Fatigue accumulates linearly every single second, acting as a direct percentage throttle on your gross yield. It is hardcapped at 99%—because if it hit 100%, the building would technically no longer exist, and we'd have to stop charging for it.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Liquidity Router (The Laundromat)</strong></summary>

Our native decentralized exchange (DEX) integration. This allows operators to seamlessly swap their **WMON** for **$RENT** (or vice-versa) directly from the OS terminal. Why leave the office to dump on the market?

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>The Oracle</strong></summary>

Our internal surveillance state. We do not rely on lagging, third-party Web2 APIs to tell us what our money is worth. The OS Oracle looks directly into the on-chain Monad liquidity pools to fetch real-time pricing down to the millisecond.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Protocol Siphon (The House Cut)</strong></summary>

The baseline 25% tax forcibly extracted from your gross yield by the `PORTFOLIO` contract. We don't put this in a treasury; the protocol immediately and permanently burns it. We set fire to your taxes to mathematically force the remaining token supply to become more scarce.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Synergy (Corporate Collusion)</strong></summary>

A hidden, highly lucrative protocol multiplier activated by staking two specific properties from complementary geographic zones (e.g., cornering the market by pairing _Foreclosure Fields_ with _Landlord Lagoon_). Monopoly building is highly encouraged.

</details>

<details>

<summary><strong>Yield</strong></summary>

The lifeblood of the empire. The exact amount of `$RENT` tokens your staked properties successfully extract from the working class per day.

</details>
