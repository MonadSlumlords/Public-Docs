# ⏱️ The 24-Hour Hustle: Quick Start

**"Welcome to the meat grinder, Junior. If you aren't extracting, you're being extracted."**

This onboarding guide is designed to get a new Slumlord authenticated, funded, and actively siphoning `$RENT` from the populace as efficiently as possible. Time is money, and every second you spend reading this is a second you aren't exploiting the working class.

Follow this exact sequence to establish your initial portfolio.

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### 🔌 Phase 1: System Boot & Authentication (Corporate Onboarding)

Before you can acquire assets, you must connect your terminal to the Monad network.

* Prepare Your Wallet: Ensure you have a standard Web3 provider (MetaMask, Rabby, Core) installed and locked.
* Connect to OS: Navigate to the Slumlords OS terminal and click the `CONNECT_NODE` button in the bottom right taskbar.
* Network Switch: The terminal will automatically prompt you to switch your network to the **Monad Mainnet**. Approve this request. We do not accept testnet monopoly money here.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### 💰 Phase 2: Capital Acquisition (Funding the War Chest)

You need liquidity to operate. Public property mints require a dual-token payment of both native **MON** and **$RENT** tokens.

* Fund Your Node: Ensure your wallet is funded with native **MON**.
* Access the DEX: Open the **SWAP** module from your disk drive navigation.
* Acquire $RENT: Use the integrated `LIQUIDITY_ROUTER` to market-buy `$RENT` using a portion of your **MON**.

{% hint style="info" %}
Note for Juniors: The router automatically handles wrapping your MON to WMON under the hood. Don't think about it too hard, just execute the swap.
{% endhint %}
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### 🏙️ Phase 3: Real Estate Acquisition (The Land Grab)

Now that you hold both assets, it's time to acquire your first concrete traps.

* Access the Registry: Open the **MINT** module.
* Target the Market: Navigate to the `PUBLIC_MINT.EXE` interface.
* Set Volume: Select your mint quantity. The terminal will display your total **MON** base cost and the total **$RENT** burn requirement (the tokens we will immediately set on fire to pump your bags).
* Sign the Papers: Click `AUTHORIZE RENT` to approve the contract's access to your tokens, then click `MINT PROPERTIES` to execute the transaction. The smart contract will randomly assign your property's Tier and District Zone.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### 🩸 Phase 4: Yield Extraction (Putting Them to Work)

Properties sitting idle in your wallet represent dead equity. At BlackCoq, hoarding idle assets is a fireable offense. You must stake them to begin extracting value.

* Open the Vault: Navigate to the **PORTFOLIO** module.
* Locate Assets: Your newly minted properties will appear in the **WALLET** tab.
* Initiate Lockup: Click `SELECT ALL`, then hit `APPROVE STAKING`.
* Turn on the Vacuum: Once the contract is authorized, click `STAKE`.

{% hint style="success" %}
STATUS: Your properties are now in the ruthless custody of the `PORTFOLIO` contract and will begin generating `$RENT` every single second.
{% endhint %}

Return to your **DASHBOARD** to watch your Unclaimed Yield reserve climb, and remember to keep an eye on your property fatigue. If the pipes burst, the yield stops. Happy hunting.
{% endstep %}
