# 🖥️ The Dashboard: Your Digital Panopticon

Welcome to your command center, Junior Slumlord. The Dashboard is your primary interface for macro-level portfolio monitoring and daily yield operations. Once your properties are staked, the OS will begin feeding live data directly to this terminal.

Think of it as your digital panopticon. From here, you can watch your tenants' hard-earned Monad convert directly into your passive income.

***

## 🩸 The Yield Reserve & Unclaimed $RENT

This module tracks your active extraction rate. Properties staked in your Portfolio continuously generate `$RENT` tokens every second.

* **Net Yield/Day:** Your daily `$RENT` accumulation. This is calculated as your gross base yield, plus any upgrade boosts, minus your active Fatigue penalty and protocol taxes.
* **Fatigue Impact:** Real estate degrades. Over time, your properties accumulate "Fatigue," which acts as a direct percentage penalty against your gross yield. (At BlackCoq, we don't fix the plumbing—we just let the yield drop until you pay the reset fee).

{% hint style="info" %}
**Claim Yield:** Click to execute a smart contract call and pull your accumulated `$RENT` into your wallet. Note: Claiming requires a blockchain transaction. Optimize your claim frequency based on current network gas conditions to preserve profit margins. Monad is fast and cheap, but we still expect you to be ruthlessly frugal.
{% endhint %}
