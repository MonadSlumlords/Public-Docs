# 🧠 Lobbying & Corruption: The Skill Tree

**"Real estate is just concrete and wood. True power is knowing who to bribe."**

**Overview**\
True portfolio optimization requires investing in the operator, not just the real estate. The Skill System allows you to permanently upgrade your account's macroeconomic parameters by essentially lobbying the Monad city council. Operators are hardcapped at a lifetime maximum of 20 Skill Points.

***

## 💼 1. Acquiring Influence (How to Buy)

Skill points are not earned through "hard work"; they are bought exclusively using your extracted `$RENT`. The `PORTFOLIO` contract utilizes a harsh quadratic bonding curve to calculate the price, heavily penalizing Slumlords trying to max out their corruption instantly.

**The Math (The Bribery Curve):**

* The baseline cost scalar is 50 `$RENT`.
* The formula for your next point is: `50 * (Current Points + 1)^2`.
* As you acquire more points, the `$RENT` cost per point scales exponentially. Your first favor costs 50 `$RENT`, but acquiring your 10th point costs a small fortune.
* Every token spent on Skill Points is instantly and permanently incinerated by the protocol.

***

## 🗃️ 2. The Allocation Desk (How to Spend)

Once purchased, points sit unallocated. They provide no benefit until you physically deploy them into your operator profile. You must allocate your points across four specific corruption parameters, with each parameter holding a hard maximum of 5 Skill Points.

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Fatigue Reduction ("Strategic Negligence")

Train your property managers to ignore urgent maintenance requests. Each point reduces your global fatigue accumulation rate by 5% (Max 25% reduction).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Yield Multiplier ("Predatory Pricing")

Artificially inflate your income just because you can. Each point adds a 5% multiplier to your gross base earnings (Max +25% boost).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Tax Reduction ("Offshore Accounting")

Hide your assets from the protocol's tax collectors. Each point lowers your effective maintenance tax rate by 3% (Max -15% reduction).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Staking Expansion ("Zoning Board Manipulation")

Change the city bylaws so you can hoard more real estate. Each point adds 2 additional staking slots to your active Portfolio (Max +10 slots).
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

**Execution Protocol:**\
In the **SKILLS** module, distribute your points and click **ALLOCATE POINTS**.

{% hint style="info" %}
The contract will automatically harvest your pending yield before applying the new multipliers to prevent time-travel exploits. We like stealing, but we prefer it to be mathematically sound.
{% endhint %}

***

## ☢️ 3. Administrative Respecs (Corporate Restructuring)

The OS allows you to reset and reallocate your Skill Points if your macro strategy changes (e.g., pivoting from Staking Expansion to Tax Reduction in the late game). However, executing a skill point respec is an extremely violent administrative action.

{% hint style="danger" %}
A respec is destructive: it carries both a steep financial cost and forceful portfolio operations. Read the penalties and protocol carefully before proceeding.
{% endhint %}

**The Financial Penalty (The Golden Parachute):**

* The contract will automatically calculate your current 24-hour daily gross yield and charge you exactly **200% (2x)** of that amount in `$RENT` to process the reset.



**The Operational Penalty (The Mass Eviction):**\
A respec does not just reset your points. To prevent Slumlords from illegally holding more properties than their new Staking Expansion level allows, the contract initiates an **AUTO-EJECT**.

When you execute a reset:

* The contract forcefully evicts all tenants and unstakes every single property in your active portfolio.
* It claims all pending yield and detaches all installed upgrades, returning everything to your wallet.
* You are left with a blank slate, a massive `$RENT` bill, and a lot of angry former tenants.
