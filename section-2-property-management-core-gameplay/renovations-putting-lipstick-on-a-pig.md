# 🛠️ Renovations: Putting Lipstick on a Pig

**"Base rent is for amateurs. Real operators nickel-and-dime their way to the top."**

Welcome back, Junior Slumlord. Base property yield is just the foundation of your empire. If you want to maximize your daily extraction and squeeze every drop of capital out of your portfolio, you need the Upgrades Module. Upgrades are standalone ERC-721 tokens that represent physical (and often highly questionable) property improvements—ranging from "Garden Renovations" all the way to the ultimate "Penthouse Suite".

***

## 🏗️ The Catalog: A Tech Tree of Urban Decay

The `UPGRADE_CATALOG.EXE` features a strict, 20-level sequential release schedule.

* **Linear Unlocking:** You can't skip to the end. The smart contract strictly enforces that Level _N_ cannot be minted until the absolute maximum supply of Level _N-1_ has been completely depleted.
* **Manufactured Scarcity:** Every upgrade tier has a hardcoded maximum supply. Once an upgrade tier is "ARCHIVED," those specific renovations are gone forever. FOMO is our favorite marketing tool.

***

## 💥 Acquisition: The Deflationary Nuke

Purchasing an upgrade isn't just a transaction; it is an aggressive, hyper-deflationary event designed to suffocate the `$RENT` circulating supply and pump your bags.

* **The Dual-Asset Squeeze:** Acquiring an upgrade requires two things: a base **MON** fee and a specific `$RENT` token payment.
* **The Automated Burn:** When you execute an upgrade mint, the contract takes your `$RENT` payment and permanently destroys it.
* **Liquidity Sweeping:** The MON is automatically routed into the decentralized exchange, market-buys `$RENT` right off the liquidity pool, and **burns that too**. Every single upgrade purchased applies massive, automated upward buy pressure on the token.

***

## 🖌️ Installation: Slapping Paint on the Walls

Upgrades sitting in your "WALLET" tab are dead weight. They generate zero yield until they are physically installed on an actively staked property.

* **Attachment:** Select an upgrade from "IN STORAGE" and hit "ATTACH". This locks the ERC-721 token into the `PORTFOLIO` contract and supercharges that property's yield.
* **Structural Limits (Slots):** A property's base Tier strictly dictates how many renovations it can physically support:
  * **Tier 1 (T1):** Maximum of **2** Upgrade Slots.
  * **Tier 2 (T2):** Maximum of **3** Upgrade Slots.
  * **Tier 3 (T3):** Maximum of **4** Upgrade Slots.

***

## 🏚️ The Golden Rule: Upgrades Rot, Too

Understanding the yield math is what separates the whales from the fish. Upgrades add a massive flat `$RENT` boost to your property, but **they are subject to the same structural decay as the building itself**.

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### 1. The Gross Calculation

The protocol first calculates your maximum theoretical output by adding the base property yield to the flat yield of all attached upgrades.

`Gross Yield = Base Yield + Total Upgrade Yield`
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### 2. The Fatigue Penalty

Once the gross yield is calculated, the contract applies your property's accumulated fatigue percentage to the _entire amount_.

`Net Yield = Gross Yield * (10000 - Fatigue BPS) / 10000`
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

The Reality Check: Upgrades are not immune to fatigue. Maintain your assets, or the protocol will eat your profits.
